Happy Clients

Simple 2 Step Solution

Step 1: Placing Order

Clients can reach us through the website, our hotline numbers, Whatsapp or Facebook messages to place the order. They need to provide us an image of their prescription in order to confirm the order.

Step 2: Receive the reports/medicine at home

As soon as the information are provided, our rider will collect the report from the lab on behalf of the clients and will hand-deliver them within 2 hours of report printing. The same case also applicable for medicine service. Clients will pay us after getting their product.
