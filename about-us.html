About Us

eCureHub is a tech company working for digitization & convenience of the healthcare industry of Bangladesh.

Considering the demand of the digitalization, eCureHub started its journey with the goal of changing the culture of the present healthcare practices by its timeliness services. The key service of eCureHub is providing medical test reports at clients’ doorsteps for a minimum charge aiming to save client’s precious time and hassle at the same time reducing the unnecessary risks in this pandemic situation.

The Unavoidable Problem

Life in most densely populated city in the world Dhaka, is hectic and busy. Transportation is both expensive and time consuming. In a city where your average movement speed is 5KM per hour, Millions of hours are wasted in the traffic on a daily basis. Everyday more than 100,000 diagnostic tests are done in Dhaka alone, which means 100,000 people needs make their trip to diagnostic centers and hospitals twice, 1st to drop their blood sample or x-ray or MRI or any other tests. 2nd to collect the reports from lab premises. Moreover, you must stand in the long queue to get your report. The 1st trip is unavoidable, since you have to be physically present for most health checkups, however it is not at all necessary for the 2nd trip. On top of these, there is a risk of coming into the contact of any kind of infectious diseases such as COVID-19.

The same problem is applicable for medicine as well.

Another big problem in Bangladesh, like most third world country is, digitization of health records. If you somehow can’t find your health records, it is near to impossible to find copies anywhere online.

The Perfect Solution

eCureHub aims on addressing all the above-mentioned problems. As a pioneer eCureHub will:

  1. Deliver your medical records AKA diagnostic reports on your doorstep.
  2. Deliver your desired medicines at home.
  3. Electronic Medical Record (EMR) on our website.

By taking our service, our clients will be able to :

  1. Avoid the 2nd unnecessary journey to diagnostic centers to collect there report, hence saving hours of time wasting.
  2. Get genuine medicines delivered at their home.
  3. Saving money by not have to make the costly journey to hospitals or medicine stores. For report delivery, we will charge only tk 80 (less than $1). Medicine delivery for our subscribed clients will be free.
  4. Electronic Medical Records (EMR) of their reports, so there is no fear of losing your health records anymore. This will be a complementary service for our users. They can also save their past health records on their account in our website
